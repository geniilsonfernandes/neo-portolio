### Overview

Zephyr is a minimalist and offline-first event timer app. It allows you to easily track and manage countdowns to important events with a sleek, responsive, and distraction-free design. Ideal for personal, academic, or professional use where simplicity and performance matter most.

![Project Cover](https://res.cloudinary.com/dbpayojb3/image/upload/v1745873085/portfolio/ygkrlxuqhj4seykcyxqi.png)

### Features

- **Offline-first**: Zephyr works offline, providing a seamless experience even when there's no internet connection.
- **Minimalist**: The app features a clean and minimal design, making it easy to navigate and stay focused on the countdowns.
- **Efficient**: Zephyr is built with performance in mind, ensuring a smooth and responsive user experience.
